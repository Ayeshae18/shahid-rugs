Our Story

Philosophy

Weaving Heritage
Into Every Thread

Founded by master weaver Shahid Karim in 1978, our atelier began with a simple belief: that a rug should outlast its owner and tell a story across generations. Starting from a single workshop in the old city of Lahore, we have grown into one of South Asia's most respected names in artisan rugs ‚Äî while never losing the intimacy and care of that first loom.

The Team

The Hands Behind the Work

Shahid Karim
Founder & Master Weaver

With over 50 years of experience, Shahid learned his craft from his father and has dedicated his life to preserving Pakistan's weaving heritage.

Ayesha Karim
Creative Director

Ayesha bridges tradition and contemporary design, curating patterns that speak to global tastes while honoring ancient motifs.

Omar Farooq
Head of Artisan Relations

Omar works directly with our network of 200+ artisan families, ensuring fair wages, safe conditions, and the preservation of traditional techniques.

Journey

Our Milestones

1978
The First Loom

Shahid Karim establishes a small workshop in Lahore's old city with two master weavers and a vision for quality above all else.

1990
Expansion to Afghanistan

We forge partnerships with Afghani tribal weavers, adding the rich tradition of Kilim and tribal weaving to our portfolio.

2001
International Recognition

Shahid Rugs wins the Asia Pacific Craft Excellence Award and begins exporting to Europe and North America.

2018
Digital Transformation

We launch our online store, bringing our collections to customers worldwide while maintaining our commitment to personal service.

2024
200+ Artisan Families

Our network now supports over 200 artisan families, providing sustainable income and preserving weaving heritage for future generations.
